Our Story

Ok so there's this girl I found cute and liked her vibes through her vlogs that she posts as Instagram stories. I first noticed her on Jan 28. On my birthday, Feb 10, to my surprise, the girl that I had a huge crush on decides to wish me. We've just seen each other on campus, never really talked. Today is Feb 27 and I see my life ahead with her. After she wished me, we've been talking ever since. We met for coffee on the 12th, then we went for a long walk in the evening. We went to the church and some other scenic spots another day. Last week we were together all the time. I have shared a lot of my life with her and she has too. She has told me about her family matters and so have I. We talked about the relationships that we want and also talked about how we both are like similar people; our interests align a lot. I genuinely think she can make me a better person in life and me vice versa...
